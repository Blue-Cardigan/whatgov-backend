Summary:
The UK government reiterated its unwavering support for Ukraine against Russian aggression, including a commitment to substantial military and financial aid packages.
Cross-party consensus was evident in the Lords, where MPs pressed for clarity on international coordination, including the release of sanctioned Russian assets and preventing trade advantages to nations supporting Russia.
The debate highlighted UK commitment to strengthening support strategies and proactively addressing concerns surrounding Ukraine's military and humanitarian needs.
