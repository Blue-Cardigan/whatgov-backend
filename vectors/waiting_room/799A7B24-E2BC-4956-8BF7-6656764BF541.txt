Summary:
The UK government expressed deep concerns over Georgian electoral irregularities and Russian interference, highlighting calls for Georgian authorities to investigate election-related issues, align with European values and safeguard democratic processes.
Cross-party discussions recognised alarming intimidation tactics by the Georgian Dream party and underscored the United Kingdom's commitment to working with international partners to counter Russian malevolent activities impacting democratic systems.
Ministerial responses committed to bolstering civil society in Georgia with efforts to protect NGOs, reinforce cybersecurity, and ensure that Georgian territorial sovereignty is respected amidst geopolitical tensions.
